App location:
/private/var/containers/Bundle/Application/

theme:
create a folder with name theme and location in /Library/Themes/
go to info.plist in app -> find <key>CFBundleIdentifier</key> copy the name from <string> </string> and rename the image to name@2x.png, name@3x.png
open the anemone and choose the theme

name@3x.png - 180-180 px
